Kreeder

The one and only speed reading plugin for Kindle Cloud Reader. Uses the RSVP technique to increase your reading speeds for all of your kindle books. Love to speed read, but hate you can't speed read kindle books. Now you can!

To use.

  1. Go to read.amazon.com
  2. Click the Kreeder Icon and click open Kreeder (Alternatively press CTRL+SHIFT+SPACE) to open the reader window
  3. Enjoy

KNOWN ISSUES:

  1. Pressing CTRL-SHIFT-SPACE (or clicking open Kreeder) repeatedly opens multiple Kreeder windows.
  2. Books with lots of images, or books with multiple layouts (e.g. Any of the Dummies books with lots of callouts), will end up with text appearing in non-sequential order. Kreeder works best with plain text books (e.g. are the words only on the pages without any special blocks or formatting).
  3. Tested on Linux and Windows
